Thread 14 — ZKPROV training-data provenance + VFT + frontier upgrade pass

WHAT
----
Tex produces zero-knowledge proofs that every model output was generated from
an authorized training-data manifest, verifiable independently in under 2
seconds without revealing the training data itself.

Five upgrades after the initial Thread 14 build push the wiring to the
actual May-18-2026 bleeding edge:

UPGRADE 1: VEIL hash-based ZK wrapper (eprint 2026/683, Apr 8 2026)
  Dalal-Hemo-Rabinovich-Rothblum compiler adds ZK to hash-based multilinear
  proof systems at ~3% prover overhead. Plausibly post-quantum on hash
  assumptions alone. ProofBackendId variant veil-hash-based-zk-2026
  reserved, regulator-grade.

UPGRADE 2: SP1 Hypercube zkVM (Succinct, mainnet Feb 19 2026)
  Multilinear-polynomial zkVM. Proves 99.7% of L1 Ethereum blocks in <12s
  on 16 RTX 5090 GPUs. Formally verified RISC-V constraints.
  ProofBackendId variant sp1-hypercube-2026 reserved, regulator-grade.

UPGRADE 3: Mira parallel folding (ZKTorch, arxiv 2507.07031)
  Tree-based homomorphic accumulation reduction. 3x-10x proof-size
  reduction, 6.2x faster proving. FoldingScheme.MIRA_PARALLEL_2026 added.

UPGRADE 4: Real Poseidon-BN254-t3 Merkle (replaces SHA-256 reduction)
  Wired the poseidon-hash PyPI package. Parameters: BN254, t=3, alpha=5,
  RF=8, RP=57, 128-bit security. SHA-256 reduction stays as fallback
  "sha256-reduced-bn254", automatically refused from regulator-grade.

UPGRADE 5: /v1/zkprov/health exposes merkle_hash_in_use + 12 pinned standards
  Added veil_hash_based_zk, sp1_hypercube, mira_parallel_accumulation,
  poseidon_merkle_hash entries with citation dates.
